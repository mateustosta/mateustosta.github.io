About me

ABOUT ME

Computer Science student and researcher

My name is Mateus Metzker Tosta, I'm 22 years old, and currently, I'm pursuing a degree in computer science at the Federal University of Paraíba (UFPB). I'm also a PIBIC-CNPQ fellow in the Petroleum Engineering Modelling Laboratory (LaMEP). My research lies at the intersection of Petroleum Engineering and Computer Science, this research aims to develop a tool for post-processing of oil reservoirs' 3D models.

PROJECTS

APyCE Project

I'm currently working on the APyCE Project, this project aims to offer an open-source alternative, in Python, for post-processing of 3D models of oil reservoirs, in addition to allowing the integration of corner-point meshes between CMG Builder® and Schlumberger Eclipse®, both widely used amidst oil and gas researchers. This project is based on the tool MRST - Matlab Reservoir Simulation Toolbox, free open-source software for reservoir modelling and simulation by SINTEF.
